"Eat food. Not too much. Mostly plants."

-- Michael Pollan

Simply good is an easy way to keep track of the whole foods you eat without having to worry about counting calories.

The guiding principle of simply good is to give people awareness of how many whole foods they are eating versus processed foods.

This is a judgment free diary.

Nutritional goals and values vary from person to person so you can use simply good to fit with your lifestyle choices.

The goal here is for you to have awareness of your dietary habits while keeping a healthy relationship with food.
